Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Implants

How much do dental implants typically cost at Jonathan A. Morris D.D.S., P.C. in Bethesda, MD?
The cost of dental implants at our Bethesda office generally ranges from $3,500 to $5,500 per implant, depending on your specific needs and whether additional procedures, like bone grafting, are required. We provide a detailed treatment plan and transparent pricing after your initial consultation. Our team can also help you explore insurance benefits and flexible payment options.
What is the typical timeline for getting a dental implant at your Bethesda practice?
The entire dental implant process usually takes between 4 to 7 months, depending on your individual case. After your consultation, we schedule the implant placement, which is followed by a healing period of 3-6 months to allow the implant to integrate with your jawbone. Once healed, we attach the custom crown. We’ll discuss your specific timeline during your first visit.
Is Dr. Jonathan A. Morris licensed and experienced in performing dental implant procedures?
Yes, Dr. Morris is a licensed dentist in Maryland with over 20 years of experience, including advanced training in implant dentistry. He regularly attends continuing education courses to stay current with the latest techniques and technology, ensuring you receive high-quality, safe care.
What should I expect during the dental implant process at your office?
Your dental implant journey begins with a comprehensive consultation and 3D imaging at our Bethesda office. If you're a candidate, the next step is surgically placing the implant under local anesthesia or sedation. After a healing period, we take impressions and custom-make your crown for a natural look. Throughout the process, we provide detailed instructions, progress checks, and attentive care.
Do dental implants come with any guarantees or warranties at Jonathan A. Morris D.D.S., P.C.?
We stand behind our implant work and offer a limited warranty on both the implant and the crown, provided you maintain regular checkups and proper oral hygiene. We’ll review the details of our warranty policy with you before treatment and are always available to address any concerns after your procedure.
